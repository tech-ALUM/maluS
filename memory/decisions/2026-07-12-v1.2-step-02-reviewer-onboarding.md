# v1.2 Step 2 — Reviewer Onboarding & Hand-off Decisions

Requested by Alberto (2026-07-11 design, 2026-07-12 implementation): once a
reviewer was assigned there was no hand-off — they had to know to log in and
find the review, and "Edit my copy" was one button among several ("Ho aggiunto
i membri e poi?").

## Observations

- [decision] The review page shows a prominent reviewer landing CTA ("Add your comments" -> the edit-copy page) when the current user's role is reviewer; this REPLACES the old "Edit my copy" action button (the banner is the hand-off, the duplicate was removed) #onboarding
- [decision] A "Copy review link" control (owner/admin only, matching the Members-button gating) copies the review URL, built client-side from a data-path + location.origin by malusCopyLink(), with a window.prompt fallback when navigator.clipboard is unavailable #onboarding
- [decision] The review list (/ui/reviews) badges reviews awaiting *my* comment: computed in reviews_page as role == reviewer AND no submitted copy (no ReviewerCopy row, or submitted_at is None); the badge clears once the reviewer submits #onboarding
- [decision] No email / SMTP (deferred, as in Step 10): the shareable link is the existing AUTHENTICATED review URL — no anonymous access; the recipient still needs their account + a reviewer seat. The link removes "where do I go?", not authentication #scope
- [context] 3 new tests in tests/web/test_onboarding.py (reviewer CTA vs owner, copy-link present for owner, "to comment" cue appears then clears on submit); full suite green (199). Live-verified in a browser: reviewer sees CTA + list badge, owner sees the copy-link which writes the correct absolute URL and flips to "Link copied" #testing
